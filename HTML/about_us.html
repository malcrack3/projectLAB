Start Grooving, Anytime, Anywhere

Founded in 2016

C’Groovy was born with a bold vision

To bring music to everyone’s fingertips and connect listeners with a world of genres and artists.

To bring music to everyone’s fingertips and connect listeners with a world of genres and artists — effortlessly, intimately, and meaningfully. Launched in 2016, C’Groovy started as a small passion project with a simple goal: to make music more accessible in the digital age. But as the world evolved and technology accelerated, so did our ambition. We came to understand that music is more than just a form of entertainment — it is a universal language that unites people, tells stories, heals, inspires, and empowers. It’s not just about what you hear — it’s about what you feel. We created C’Groovy to be more than just another streaming platform. We wanted to build a personalized music experience that adapts to you — your taste, your mood, your moment. Whether you're dancing to the latest pop hits, zoning in with lo-fi beats, or rediscovering childhood classics, C’Groovy is here to soundtrack your life.

Our Philosophy Music, Reimagined

We believe that music should be

Accessible

Everyone deserves great music. That’s why we work tirelessly to make our platform lightweight, intuitive, and available across all devices.

Personal

Your musical taste is as unique as your fingerprint. C’Groovy learns from your listening habits, crafting playlists and suggestions that feel just right — because no two users are the same.

Empowering

Whether you're a listener or an artist, we want to uplift voices and help music travel further. We support independent creators and emerging scenes, giving them the spotlight they deserve.

Inclusive

From Tokyo to Lagos, we bring together musical cultures and bridge communities. Music is borderless, and so are we.

What Makes C’Groovy Different?

Tailored Discovery

We use smart algorithms powered by real-time learning to help you explore songs and artists that match your vibe. Forget endless scrolling — your next favorite song is just one suggestion away.

Global Sound, Local Vibes

We work with local curators and trendsetters to uncover the heart of regional music scenes. Whether it’s K-Pop from Seoul, Afrobeat from Lagos, or Samba from Rio — we bring the world to your ears.

Seamless Everywhere

Your music goes wherever you do. C’Groovy works smoothly across smartphones, tablets, desktops, and even offline — so the beat never stops, even when the Wi-Fi does.

Intelligent and Intuitive

Built with human-centered design and backed by AI, our platform makes discovering music effortless. From mood playlists to genre journeys, every feature is crafted to enhance your experience — not complicate it.
